About BeginGently

Why this guide exists, and how it's built.

BeginGently (صدقة) means "charitable gift." This entire app is built as ṣadaqah jāriyah (صدقة جارية)— a lasting charity that continues to benefit others. It exists for one purpose: to help reverts and sincere seekers find peace and clarity on their journey to Islam.

Why We Built This

Many reverts feel lost.

There's an ocean of Islamic content out there—but much of it assumes existing knowledge, uses complex language, or can feel overwhelming. New Muslims often feel judged, afraid, or unsure where to start.

Fear of mistakes is a barrier.

The path to Islam shouldn't feel scary. But fear-based messaging, complex fiqh, and perfectionism push people away. What reverts need is reassurance and clarity.

Content is scattered.

There's no single, calm, beginner-friendly guide. This app brings together essential knowledge—Qur'an, Salah, Beliefs, Prophet's life, Hadith, Practice—all in one gentle place.

Our Core Principles

1. Gentleness Over Force

No guilt, no "you must" language. This app meets you where you are and invites you to take one small step at a time.

2. Sincerity Over Perfection

Allah sees your heart. Mistakes are normal. This guide normalizes imperfection and celebrates the courage it took to begin.

3. Clarity Without Ideology

We avoid debates, sectarian divisions, and complex fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence). We use widely accepted, trusted sources and focus on what unites Muslims.

4. Simplicity in Design & Content

Minimal design, lots of white space, soft colors, and one clear action per page. Content is short, contextual, and digestible.

5. Safety Over Completeness

Emotional safety matters more than covering every topic. We avoid fatwas, controversial positions, and complex theological debates.

6. Learning Over Speed

There's no rush. Islam is a lifelong journey. This guide celebrates gradual growth and reflection over quick fixes.

Who This Is For

This app is built for:

People who've just embraced Islam and feel overwhelmed by the breadth of knowledge ahead.

Sincere seekers exploring Islam and wanting to understand it clearly.

People afraid of making mistakes and needing reassurance that learning is gradual.

Anyone seeking calm, non-judgmental guidance without pressure or sectarian debate.

Accessibility & Free to Use

This app is completely free. No ads, no subscriptions, no tracking. It's built as charity for anyone seeking to learn about Islam.

Designed for mobile-first. Whether you're reading on a phone, tablet, or desktop, Sadaqa is calm, clear, and easy to navigate.
