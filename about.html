About BeginGently

Why this guide exists, and how it's built.

BeginGently is a free, comprehensive Islamic guide built specifically for Muslim reverts and seekers. This entire resource is created as Sadaqah Jariyah (صدقة جارية)—ongoing charity that continues to benefit people for years to come. It exists for one clear purpose: to help reverts and sincere seekers find peace, clarity, and confidence on their journey to Islam, with gentle guidance every step of the way.

What Does "BeginGently" Mean?

"BeginGently" reflects our philosophy: that accepting Islam and learning to practice it should be gentle, gradual, and judgment-free. You don't need to know everything immediately. You don't need to be perfect. You just need to begin sincerely, with patience and peace. That's the BeginGently approach.

Why We Built This: The Challenge Reverts Face

Muslim reverts share common struggles:

Accepting Islam is a profound decision—but many new Muslims find themselves isolated, overwhelmed, or unsure of their path. The challenges are real and understandable.

Many reverts feel lost and confused

There's an overwhelming ocean of Islamic content out there. But much of it assumes you already know Arabic, understand Islamic tradition, or have studied extensive theology. New Muslims often feel judged, afraid of making mistakes, or unsure where to start. They need a beginner-friendly guide—something that acknowledges their starting point.

Fear and perfectionism create barriers

The path to Islam shouldn't feel scary. But the reality is that some Islamic content emphasizes rules, consequences, and perfectionism— which can push new Muslims away or make them feel inadequate. What reverts truly need is reassurance, gentle guidance, and the knowledge that learning is gradual.

Islamic knowledge is scattered and hard to find

There's no single, calm, beginner-friendly guide that covers the essentials. Resources are fragmented across books, websites, and videos. BeginGently brings together the core knowledge new Muslims actually need—Qur'an, Salah, Islamic beliefs, Prophet Muhammad, Hadith, and daily practice—all in one gentle, accessible place.

Our Core Principles

1. Gentleness Over Force

There's no guilt-tripping here. No "you must" language. No pressure. BeginGently meets you exactly where you are and gently invites you to take one small step at a time. That's how learning should feel.

2. Sincerity Over Perfection

Allah looks at your heart, not your flawless performance. Mistakes are part of learning. BeginGently normalizes imperfection and celebrates the profound courage it took for you to begin this journey. You're doing great.

3. Clarity Without Ideology

We avoid sectarian debates, complex jurisprudence (fiqh), and controversial theological positions. Instead, we use widely accepted, trusted Islamic sources and focus on what unites Muslims across different traditions.

4. Simplicity in Design & Content

Everything here is intentionally simple. Minimal design, plenty of white space, soft, welcoming colors, and content that's short, clear, and digestible. No overwhelming text walls. No confusing jargon. Just clear guidance.

5. Emotional Safety Above All

Your emotional wellbeing matters more than covering every imaginable topic. We intentionally avoid issuing fatwas (religious rulings), controversial positions, and complex theological debates that might confuse or discourage you.

6. Learning Over Speed

There's no rush. Islam is a lifelong journey of growth and reflection. We celebrate gradual progress over quick fixes. One sincere prayer today is better than rushing through five prayers with resentment.

Who This Is For

This app is built for:

Muslim reverts (people who've embraced Islam) who feel overwhelmed by the breadth of knowledge ahead and want a clear starting point.

Sincere seekers exploring Islam and wanting authentic, beginner-friendly information without judgment.

People afraid of making mistakes in prayer, Islamic practice, or belief—and who need reassurance that learning is gradual.

Anyone seeking calm, non-judgmental guidance on Islamic topics, without pressure, perfectionism, or sectarian debate.

Accessibility & Free to Use

This app is completely free. No ads, no subscriptions, no tracking. It's built as charity for anyone seeking to learn about Islam.

Designed for mobile-first. Whether you're reading on a phone, tablet, or desktop, Sadaqa is calm, clear, and easy to navigate.

In service of the Ummah. This guide is maintained by Muslim students and volunteers who care deeply about the success of Muslim reverts. For feedback, questions, or suggestions, please reach out. Your voice matters.
