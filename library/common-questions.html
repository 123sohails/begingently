Common Questions

A gentle Q&A for things people often ask when they first embrace Islam.

How do I pray when I-m travelling?

When you are travelling (over ~48 miles/77 km) you may shorten and combine certain prayers according to the Qur-an (4:101) and Prophetic practice. Dhuhr with Asr, and Maghrib with Isha can be prayed together. Make sure you still face the qibla and try to pray as soon as you can.

What if I forget a prayer?

Simply perform it as soon as you remember and say -sallallahu alayhi wa sallam- and make up the missed rak-Sahs--the intention (niyyah) is key.

Can I fast if I have diabetes?

Health comes first. If fasting seriously harms you, you are excused and can make up days later or give charity (fidya) instead. Consult a doctor and a trusted scholar.
