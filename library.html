How to Use This Library

There's no "right" order. Start wherever feels right to you. Many reverts begin with Salah (prayer), but others start with understanding beliefs or reading the Qur'an.

Take your time. This is a journey, not a race. Spend as much time as you need on each section. Re-read things. Reflect. Ask questions (in your heart, in your community, slowly).

Mistakes are learning. If you get something wrong, that's fine. That's how we grow.
